Union Square Sessions

Union Square Sessions are a session band, compromised of professional musicians from the UK. With over 30+ combined years of performing across the UK, Europe and USA.

With a base group of four members ( Drums/BVs, Bass/BVs, Guitar/BVs, Guitar/Lead Vocals) Union Square Sessions are all about the music and providing the best entertainment for your event.

From The clash to Queen, lady Gaga to the Beastie Boys and everything in between; Union Square Sessions provide a night of high energy entertainment from the first note to the last chord. With concert-level stage production and a disco/DJ provided as standard. All this means that every possible aspect of your evening’s entertainment is taken care of.
